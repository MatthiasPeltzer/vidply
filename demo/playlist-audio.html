Features Demonstrated

  • Previous/Next buttons - Navigate between tracks
  • Auto-advance - Automatically plays the next track
  • Track info display - Shows current track number and title
  • Playlist panel - Click any track to play it
  • Album art - Thumbnail images for each track
  • Per-track captions - Some tracks have lyrics/captions
  • Active track highlighting - Shows which track is currently playing
  • Keyboard navigation - Use Tab and Enter to navigate the playlist

Perfect For

  • Music Players - Albums, playlists, artist collections
  • Podcasts - Episode series
  • Audiobooks - Chapter navigation
  • Audio Courses - Lesson sequences
  • Language Learning - Audio lessons with transcripts
